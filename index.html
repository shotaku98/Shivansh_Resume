Hello, My Name is Shivansh.


I am a result-oriented Mechanical Engineer with a masters from Northwestern University and 2+ years of cross-functional experience in Product Development, CAD, FEA, and CFD. Proficient in product design, manufacturing, and simulation, with a track record of optimizing product performance and driving process improvement, I am skilled in diverse modeling and simulation software.

I have worked on product development from concept phase to production, utilizing concepts of designing, design for manufacturing and assembly, GD&T, structural, fluid and thermal analysis while building FMEAs and DVP&R, automation of optimization, and hold a Six Sigma Yellow Belt certification.

I am passionate about mechanical engineering and am constantly upskilling to come up with the best solutions to engineering problems. Gaining managerial skills through an engineering management minor during masters, projects, and leadership positions, I am confident about my abilities to drive innovation at work!

My skills

Proficient in a variety of Computer-Aided Design (CAD) tools, including SolidWorks, Siemens NX, Fusion 360, Inventor, AutoCAD, CATIA, Creo, DFA, DFM, and GD&T, I bring precision and creativity to the modeling process. My expertise extends to simulation techniques, utilizing tools such as ANSYS (Fluent, Structural, Thermal), Star CCM+, Abaqus/CAE, modeFrontier.

My core competency lies in product development, where I seamlessly integrate skills in CAD and simulation to bring ideas to life. With a strong foundation in prototyping, manufacturing, and materials science, coupled with a deep understanding of mechanical systems, I am well-equipped to navigate the complexities of engineering projects, ensuring innovation and excellence from conception to realization.
